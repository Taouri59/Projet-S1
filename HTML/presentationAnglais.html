The IT formation

The IT departments of the UTI of France train students in computer science and teamwork in order to integrate them into professional life.

They offer a training on a human scale, supervised by available teachers, as well as by experienced professionals who bring students practical and concrete experience in the IT jobs.

The teaching in computer science (DUT or Professional License) is based on 3 axes, each of them requires specific qualities and skills :

-Analysis and design :

Preliminary work in relation with the client to specify his need and expectations and the design of a computer processing scheme. Communication, open-mindedness, analytical and synthesis skills...

-Algorithmics and programming :

Effective implementation of the processing up to the coding. Logical mind, technical precision...

-System architecture and networks :

Operation of a computer, its operating system and networks (Internet, Intranet, ...). Curiosity, patience...

To these targeted courses are added half of the disciplines of general education (Mathematics, Economics and Management, English and Communication, ...). This gives the training a multidisciplinary aspect. In addition, the evaluation is done not only on the theory but also on scenarios, via tutored projects and an internship in company, allowing to validate the knowledge and skills acquired during the training.
