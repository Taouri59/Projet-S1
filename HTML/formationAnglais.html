The IT formation

University technology institutes

The 115 University Institutes of Technology in France are spread over the whole territory, which ensures a strong anchorage in the local and regional economic environment. Meetings between department heads of UTIs ensure the national coherence of education.
The IUT has a quality of university education recognized and praised by companies for more than 40 years, thanks to the establishment of programs adapted to the professional reality and the creation of degrees corresponding to the creations of new jobs.
The training courses covering most sectors (secondary and tertiary) and offering the collaboration of professionals make the strength and success of UTI, and in fact allow students to find a job within 6 months after the award of the degree.
This diversification of degrees and training leads to the establishment of different pedagogical methods, which satisfy a greater number of students and professionals wishing to resume studies or make a validation of prior experience (VAE).
In addition, UTI, in partnership with foreign universities, offer courses abroad, as well as internships.

IT departments at the UTI

The teaching of computer science at the IUT plays a large part in the professional dimension while retaining fundamental components; the student acquires skills but also knowledge, basic concepts and working methods which he will be able to use in a professional situation
The DUT, university and professional degree (level L2 of the LMD scheme, 120 European credits):
Informatics DUT holders are senior technicians who are able to participate in the design, construction and implementation of computer systems. They may, however, if they wish to continue their studies at the end of this degree.
The Professional License «Computer Systems and Software» (SIL), degree of level L3 in the scheme LMD (degree of License, 180 European credits): these pages present the professional licenses offered in the UTI IT departments.
The SIL licence holders are specialists who are able to participate in a software development project and, in the long term, take full responsibility for it.
Validation of prior learning :
It is also possible to have your experience (professional or not) recognized in order to obtain a degree, a title or a certificate of professional qualification. You can visit the Government’s Validation of Experience (VAE) website.
