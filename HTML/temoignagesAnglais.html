Testimony

Discover the testimony of Damien Lefebvre

His science degree in his pocket, Damien Lefebvre spent a few months on the benches of the college, before reorienting himself, in search of a more supervised training. His academic experience having shown him a strong taste for computer science, he decided to apply to the UTI of Villetaneuse in order to prepare an IT DUT option software engineering in 2002.

The supervision offered by passionate teachers, the acquisition of rigorous working methods encouraged him to complete his training via the Professional License Computer Systems and Software option Software Engineering, offered by the same UTI.

"The knowledge and skills developed by three years of study of algorithmics, databases, but also the accounting aspects of the world of the Company, were the key that allowed me to be recruited by a large French IT service company, after 4 months of internship at the end of the year."
